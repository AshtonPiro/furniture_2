    • Type of Storage Needed

    The kind of clothes to be stored within the wardrobe also decides the type of wardrobe to be bought. If one mainly uses clothes that need to be hung rather than folded, a cabinet with more hanging space is required over one with more drawers or shelves. Items like coats, suits, sarees, gowns, floor-length dresses, etc., need more hanging-type storage capacity rather than folded storage. 

    • Wardrobe Aesthetics

    The wardrobe style must also be carefully selected to match the concept to the bedroom theme. A traditional wardrobe design will be unsuitable for a contemporary-styled bedroom and vice versa. Check out the varying designs available and choose accordingly.

    • Wardrobe Material

     The wardrobe material may be chosen as desired from wood, particle board, metal, etc. Confirm the durability and sturdiness of the materials used before buying the wardrobe online . Make sure the wardrobe is made of wood if the bed and other bedroom furniture are made from wood. The colour of the cabinet also influences the bedroom look. So make sure the most appropriate colour and finish is chosen too. 

    • Wardrobe Price

    Investing in a good-quality wardrobe is essential for long-lasting use.     You may be a fashionista spending a lot of time purchasing the best clothes and quality wardrobe. But a lack of proper wardrobe care and deep clean can cost you a lot more than you think. Let us explore some thumb rules to take care of your wardrobe below:

    • Dusting and vacuuming on a daily/weekly basis can get rid of unwanted dust.
    • Clean all the corners and angles with a dry cotton swab/cloth. Refrain from using an abrasive sponge to safeguard your wooden wardrobe. 
    • If you have a wooden wardrobe, make sure they are devoid of moisture, especially during the rainy season.
    • Invest in a quality wardrobe cover to keep your steel/wooden wardrobe dustproof, especially when you are out of town.
    • Always keep dry clothes inside the wardrobe to keep moisture and fungal growth at bay.
    • Keep your closet away from the wall to make them free from dampness and moulds.
    • Occasionally, coat the wooden wardrobe with a liquid polish to highlight the natural lustre and make it resistant to termite attacks.
    • Put camphor in smelly wooden cabinets to absorb smell and moisture while preventing microorganism proliferation. 
    • Once in a while, you can do a professional cleaning with proper cleaning agents for deep cleaning.
